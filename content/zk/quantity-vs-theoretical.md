Some say, ‘Quality Over Quantity’ is false wisdom. But It will always be for me.

They argue, that in order for you to be good at something (let’s say writing), you need to write and practice a lot. I agree. But Isn’t being of (good) Quality a by-product of extensive work too?

I guess both can’t be compared, as the one is the process(Quantity) and the other is the result(Quality).
