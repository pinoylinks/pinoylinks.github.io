Write! Write! Write! 

It doesn't matter whether it's coherent or not.

Lengthy or not.

Meaningful or not.

Just write.

E-VE-RY-DAY.

Make a regular habit of it.

Writing is a skill.

Cultivate. 

Or it withers and gets blown away.
