Contrary to what people often assume, freedom is not as simple as it seems. It’s not a one-dimensional metric that you can put a value on.

Rather, in many cases, it’s a trade-off. Pushing back my seat in a bus limits the space of someone behind me.

My increased freedom often decreases freedom for others to some degree.
