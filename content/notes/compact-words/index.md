1. **Tunnel vision** – Narrow focus, ignoring broader context.  
2. **Overthinking** – Excessive rumination or analysis.  
3. **Echo chamber** – Closed environment reinforcing specific ideas.  
4. **Glass ceiling** – Invisible barrier limiting advancement.  
5. **Snowball effect** – Small events escalating rapidly.  
6. **Ripple effect** – Small action causing widespread impact.  
7. **Butterfly effect** – Tiny changes influencing large outcomes.  
8. **Goldilocks zone** – Perfect balance or ideal conditions.  
9. **Cognitive dissonance** – Mental conflict from contradictory beliefs.  
10. **Moral hazard** – Risk-taking encouraged by safety nets.  
11. **Analysis paralysis** – Overanalyzing preventing decisions.  
12. **Blind spot** – Unseen personal weakness or ignorance.  
13. **Gaslighting** – Manipulating someone to doubt reality.  
14. **Self-fulfilling prophecy** – Belief causing its own realization.  
15. **Pareto principle** – 80/20 rule: small causes with large effects.  
16. **Boiling frog** – Gradual changes leading to unnoticed danger.  
17. **Imposter syndrome** – Feeling unworthy despite success.  
18. **Catch-22** – Paradoxical situation with no escape.  
19. **Downward spiral** – Deterioration that worsens itself.  
20. **Confirmation bias** – Seeking evidence supporting preconceptions.  
21. **Herd mentality** – Following the majority without critical thinking.  
22. **Limiting beliefs** – Self-imposed mental barriers to growth.  
23. **Big brother** – Surveillance or authoritative oversight.  
24. **Black swan** – Rare and unpredictable event with major impact.  
25. **Achilles' heel** – Vulnerable point in an otherwise strong entity.  
26. **Tipping point** – The moment of critical change or momentum shift.  
27. **Opportunity cost** – Benefits lost from not choosing an alternative.  
28. **First mover advantage** – Benefits gained by being the pioneer.  
29. **Zero-sum game** – Gains at another’s expense with no net benefit.  
30. **Melting pot** – Diverse cultures blending into one.  
31. **Brain drain** – Talent leaving for better opportunities.  
32. **Silent majority** – A large, quiet group with significant influence.  
33. **Cultural inertia** – Resistance to change in traditional practices.  
34. **Ivory tower** – Isolation from real-world concerns or practicalities.  
35. **Bystander effect** – Inaction caused by assuming others will act.  
36. **Peter principle** – Rising to one’s level of incompetence.  
37. **Skin in the game** – Personal investment in an outcome.  
38. **Midas touch** – Exceptional success in everything undertaken.  
39. **Echo boom** – Rebirth of trends or ideas after their decline.  
40. **Grassroots movement** – Change initiated by ordinary people, not leaders.
